## Late-April access cheat-sheet (PNW)
- SR 20 (N Cascades Hwy) **closed** Silver Star → Ross Dam until ~late May
- Cascade River Rd: **open to gate ~1 mi short of Cascade Pass TH** in shoulder season (past Eldorado TH). Eldorado/Boston Basin/Sahale-arm approaches in play. Adds ~1 mi walk to Cascade Pass.
- Mountain Loop Hwy through-road **closed** — Sloan/Three Fingers approaches limited
- Phelps Creek (Maude/7FJ/Fernow) — gate often closed, adds ~3 mi
- **Open year-round / early-season friendly:**
  - US 2 → Icicle Creek Rd (Stuart Range from Mountaineer Creek / Colchuck)
  - US 97 → Ingalls Creek (Stuart south side / Cascadian Couloir)
  - Teanaway Rd (opens early-mid April typically) → Esmeralda Basin
  - Mt Baker Hwy (Shuksan / Sulphide approach via Shannon Cr)
  - Methow Valley low-elev THs (Wolf Creek for Gardners — covered 4/20)

## Late-April day-trip Bulger sweet spot
Best candidates when there's a high-pressure refreeze window:
1. **Mount Stuart (9,415')** via Cascadian Couloir from Ingalls Creek — long but classic
2. **Mount Shuksan (9,131')** via Sulphide Glacier — long, glaciated
3. Stuart Range satellites (Argonaut, Colchuck, Dragontail) when conditions align
